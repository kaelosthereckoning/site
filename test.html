Table - Power Odds

Data description

Data may be sorted by column ascending / descending.
Probabilities generated using: 1-hypgeom.dist(S-1, T+7 , A, 75, true) where S = minimum influence / power needed, T = turn number, A = # of potential successes in deck. This formula gives the sum of all probabilities representing the target number or greater successes.
Card # selected using smallest whole number which generated a probability of over desired %.
