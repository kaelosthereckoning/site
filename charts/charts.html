Unstable Form - Odds of Generating Any Card of Given Power + Rarity

# Power Cards Required for 60-90% Odds of Playing Card On Curve

First Playable Turn w/ 25 Power Deck, 60/70/80/90% Odds

Data description

Probabilities generated using: 1-hypgeom.dist(S-1, T+7 , A, 75, true) where S = minimum influence / power needed, T = turn number, A = # of potential successes in deck. This formula gives the sum of all probabilities representing the target number or greater successes.
Basic assumption is 25 power cards in 75 card deck; no Warp cards or other cost- / turn-altering effects.

Last update: July 20, 2017
